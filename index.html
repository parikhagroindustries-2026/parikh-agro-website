Who We Are

A Legacy of Purity and Trust

Pioneering Healthier Cooking Solutions

At Parikh Agro Industries, we have committed ourselves to manufacturing premium quality edible oils under our flagship brand MADHAV. Based in Dahod, Gujarat, our modern processing unit handles advanced refining operations under stringent quality frameworks.

We blend legacy values with modern state-of-the-art technological setups to serve clean, healthy, and highly nutritious cooking oils across local and national markets. Our product development lifecycle ensures optimal nutrient retention while delivering high smoke points suitable for diverse culinary applications.

Our Quality Policy

At Parikh Agro Industries, quality acts as our operational foundation. We continuously strive to align raw processing frameworks with national and global food safety norms.

01

Purity Assurance

We maintain raw seed sourcing chains to process products strictly free from external adulteration and synthetic modifiers.

02

Food Safety

Our automated systems operate inside protected physical clean-rooms to adhere strictly to hygiene benchmarks.

03

Quality Testing

Testing is conducted during production intervals to check shelf stability, clarity, flavor profiles, and heat thresholds.

04

Hygienic Packaging

We use premium food-grade packaging materials, including hermetically sealed HDPE tins, pet bottles, and rigid jars, to prevent structural leakage and oxidation.

05

Customer Commitment

We maintain complete supply visibility, standardizing quality parameters in every batch shipped to institutional and retail buyers.
